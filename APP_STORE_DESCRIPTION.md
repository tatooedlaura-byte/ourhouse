## Description

Ours Lists is the simple way for families to stay organized together. Share grocery lists, track household chores, set reminders, and manage projects - all in one app that syncs instantly with your family.

**GROCERIES**
• Create multiple shopping lists
• Quickly add items with one tap
• Check off items as you shop
• See frequently bought items for easy re-adding
• Sort by category to navigate the store faster

**CHORES**
• Set up recurring chores (daily, weekly, monthly)
• Add one-time tasks for things that just need to get done
• See what's due today at a glance
• Track when chores were last completed
• Assign tasks to family members

**REMINDERS**
• Schedule reminders for specific dates each month
• Perfect for bills, pet medications, filter changes
• Monthly, quarterly, or yearly recurrence
• Get notified on the day something is due

**PROJECTS**
• Organize bigger tasks into projects
• Break down home improvements or events into steps
• Set priorities and due dates
• Archive completed projects

**SHARE WITH FAMILY**
• Invite your spouse or family members with one tap
• Everyone sees the same lists in real-time
• Changes sync instantly through iCloud
• No accounts to create - just uses your Apple ID

**PRIVACY FIRST**
• All data stored in your personal iCloud
• No tracking or analytics
• No ads
• We never see your data
